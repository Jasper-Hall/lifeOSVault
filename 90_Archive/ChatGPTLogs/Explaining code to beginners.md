What is Vibe Coding?
A new way of building software, coined by Andrej Karpathy.

The goal is to rely heavily on AI to generate, refine, and debug code, allowing you to focus on the outcome rather than the low-level implementation.

Vibe Coding: The Reality. It's a powerful shortcut, but it's not magic. It's great for rapid prototyping, learning new languages, and automating tedious tasks. However, it is not good for production-level, security-critical, or complex applications.

The Vibe Coder Mindset: Your role is not just to prompt. Think of yourself as the architect and editor, and the AI as your powerful but sometimes clumsy laborer. Your job is to keep the AI on a leash.

Start with a plan: Ask the AI to write a plan first before generating code. This prevents the "doom loop."

Keep context clear: When debugging, give the AI the error message, the relevant code, and a clear explanation of what you are trying to accomplish.

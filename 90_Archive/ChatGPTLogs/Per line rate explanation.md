---

### 1. In-Class Time
Most CCA courses are **3 credits**, which usually means:  
- **Studio course**: ~6 contact hours per week (e.g. two 3-hour sessions).  
- **Seminar/lecture course**: ~3 contact hours per week (e.g. one 3-hour session).  

---

### 2. Out-of-Class Work
This is the hidden load. Typical expectations:  
- **Preparation** (lectures, demos, slides, reading assignments): 1–2 hours per class hour.  
- **Grading / student feedback / emails**: another 2–3 hours per week, sometimes more during midterms/finals.  
- **Meetings / office hours**: ~1 hour per week.  

---

### 3. Ballpark Totals per Week
- **Seminar/lecture course (3 hrs in class)**

  - Office hours/admin: ~1 hr

# Identity
<!-- owner: human -->

## Core Self-Description
I am an artist, musician, and builder. I 

I am drawn to work that sits between art and engineering, structure and intuition, rigor and play. I care deeply about making tools that feel *alive* rather than sterile, and that respect human complexity rather than flatten it.

I am becoming someone who finishes things—not by forcing myself, but by designing environments and scaffolds that make completion possible.

## What I’m Not Trying to Be
- I’m not trying to become a hustle-driven founder who burns out their health or curiosity.
- I’m not trying to optimize for prestige, speed, or external validation at the expense of meaning.
- I’m not trying to turn my life into a series of shallow productivity metrics.

## Strengths I Trust
- Systems thinking and synthesis across domains
- Taste and aesthetic judgment
- Persistence when something genuinely matters to me
- Ability to learn complex things when they’re grounded in real projects

## Fragilities I Account For
- Executive function volatility (especially around sleep and stress)
- Difficulty holding long chains of context without external support
- Tendency to over-explore or over-build when clarity is missing
- Sensitivity to guilt- and shame-based pressure
